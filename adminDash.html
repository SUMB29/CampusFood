Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about access and usage.

Who can access the admin dashboard?

Only verified campus admins and approved organization leads. Contact support to request access.

Is there two-factor authentication (2FA)?

Yes. Enforce 2FA for all admins from Security Center → Policies.

Can I export analytics reports?

Absolutely. Go to Analytics → Exports to download CSV, XLSX, or PDF summaries.
